1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, improve your user experience, and track website performance.

2. How We Use Cookies

At Easy Craft for Beginners, we use cookies to:

  • Provide relevant content and promotions

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our site (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences and choices to enhance your experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: May be used to deliver personalized content or ads, if enabled in the future.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. You may also disable cookies completely, although some features of our website may not function properly as a result.
