Will it be safe for me to make soap at home?

Every time! The soap-making methods found in the Ultimate Soap Making Guides & Recipes are absolutely harmless to people, surfaces, plants, and animals. However, during the soap-making process, we will also show you what precautions to take to ensure that the work is 100% safe for you.

How long will it take me to create the soap?

It takes from 10 to 30 minutes to prepare the soap, depending on the level of experience of the person working. After mixing all the ingredients following the instructions and tutorials step by step, you need to wait from 24 to 72 hours for the soap to solidify and be used like a regular soap bar. The setting time depends on the temperature of the place where the soap is made: the higher the temperature, the less time it takes for the ingredients to solidify.
